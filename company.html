Our Story

Once upon a time, in the heart of Paris, three friends were looking to bring greenery into their small studios. Unfortunately, in this harsh urban environment, miniature garden shops didnâ€™t exist. From this realization, The Tree Musketeers was born in 2018, in a small space in the 14th arrondissement of Paris. The first three floral creations to see the light of day were "D'Artagnan", "Porthos", and "Aramis". They were an immediate success. After a few years of thriving in the Parisian market and opening five boutiques, the concept expanded internationally with stores in New York, London, and Sydney. Today, thousands of mini-plants are sold in boutiques and exported worldwide. The growth of The Tree Musketeers is only just beginning.
